# How to teach yourself anything?

> Before you in anything:
> - Answer these questions first: what will i be able to do that i can't do now? And how will i prove it?

1. start try to build simple app, shoot 20-30 pic and edit it then see if it good/bad. the result not good so find others resources.

2. Don't spend more than an hour to find resources, not even minute after that.

3. pick one primary resources , one backup one. point is start as soon as possible.

4. schedule a checklist for tracking.

5. understand enough to explain it alone and simplify clear to your own understanding.

6. Test yourself constantly even no one guide you.

7. build a project to apply what you learn, and share it with others.

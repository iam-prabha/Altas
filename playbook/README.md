# The SaaS Playbook

From idea to production — a tactical guide for building SaaS products people pay for.

## How to use this

Each chapter is **standalone** but builds on the ones before it. Read them in order, or jump to whatever stage you're in right now.

Every chapter follows the same format:

```
The Principle  →  a 2-paragraph story or insight
The Checklist  →  numbered actions you can take today
Case Study     →  how a real SaaS company did it
Red Flags      →  common mistakes to avoid
```

The `templates/` folder has ready-to-use worksheets (customer interview guide, pricing worksheet, smoke-test outline, weekly metrics tracker).

## Who this is for

- Technical founders who can build but need business direction
- Non-technical founders who need both business and technical context
- Anyone who wants to ship a SaaS product people actually pay for

## The Golden Rule

**Build for a specific pain, not a general idea. Charge before you scale. Talk to customers before you write code.**

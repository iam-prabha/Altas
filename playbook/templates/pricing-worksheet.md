## 7. Validation Test

- [ ] Launch with proposed pricing.
- [ ] After 20 paying customers, calculate average revenue per customer.
- [ ] Ask churned customers: "Was price a factor?"
- [ ] Ask 5 happiest customers: "Would you pay double?"
- [ ] Adjust pricing every 3-6 months until you see:
  - Conversion rate >2%
  - Churn rate <5%
  - "It's a bit expensive but worth it" feedback

## Pricing Principles

1. **You can always lower prices.** Raising them is harder. Start higher than you think.
2. **Price for the value, not the cost.** Your costs don't matter to the customer.
3. **Grandfather early customers.** They took a risk on you. Reward them.
4. **Test publicly.** Announce price changes. See what happens. Iterate.
5. **Don't compete on price.** The cheapest product wins only if everything else is equal — and it never is.

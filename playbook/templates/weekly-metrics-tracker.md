# Weekly Metrics Tracker

Track these every Monday morning. Consistent tracking beats perfect tracking.

---

## How to Use

- Update the table every Monday before doing anything else.
- If a metric is flat or declining for 3+ weeks, investigate.
- If a metric improves, ask "what caused it?" and do more of that.
- Share this with your co-founder or team (a shared Google Sheet works).

## Principles

- **Weekly > monthly.** By the time you see a monthly trend, you've lost a month.
- **Action beats analysis.** If a metric is bad, pick one action, try it, measure next week.
- **Share publicly.** Tracking in public (Indie Hackers, Twitter) keeps you accountable.
- **Don't chase vanity.** Total registered users is nice. MRR and active users are real.
- **Trends > snapshots.** One bad week is noise. Four bad weeks is a signal.
